About Us

WENDY's Restaurant opened its doors in May 2000. Our cuisine is characterized by constant transformation, and centered on three key elements of Italian and Mexican culture - corn, seafood, burgers, pasta, and vegetables. We use different techniques from Italian and Mexican heritage and approach them with a contemporary perspective. Our philosophy is to work with the highest quality producers and artisans, a deep commitment to sustainability, and providing a unique hospitality experience that feels distinctively Italian and Mexican.
